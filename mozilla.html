Install Mozilla Thunderbird & Set Up Your Email

Step-by-Step User Guide

  1. Download Mozilla Thunderbird
    • Visit: https://www.thunderbird.net
    • Click "Download" and select your operating system (Windows, macOS, Linux).
    • Run the installer and follow the prompts to complete installation.
  2. Configure Your Email in Thunderbird
    • Open Thunderbird.
    • If prompted, choose "Set up an existing email account".
    • Enter your details:

    • Click "Continue". Thunderbird will attempt automatic configuration.
    • If needed, click "Manual config" to input server details provided by your email provider.

  3. Verify Setup
    • Ensure emails are syncing properly.
    • Start managing your email via Thunderbird.

Note: For manual setup, ensure you have the correct IMAP/POP3 and SMTP server details from your email provider.
